How can I become an agent on Velki? ‡¶ï‡¶ø‡¶≠‡¶æ‡¶¨‡ßá ‡¶Ü‡¶Æ‡¶ø ‡¶≠‡ßá‡¶≤‡ßç‡¶ï‡¶ø ‡¶§‡ßá ‡¶è‡¶ú‡ßá‡¶®‡ßç‡¶ü ‡¶π‡¶§‡ßá ‡¶™‡¶æ‡¶∞‡¶ø?

To become an agent, need to contact our super agents.

Buying and selling points:

** Agent will buy points from Velki for 60 taka.
** Agent will sell to the user for 100 taka.
** Here the agent's profit will be 40%

Again:
** Agent will buy points from the user for 100 taka.
** Agent will sell to Velki for 60 taka.
** Here the agent's loss will be 40% - but here is the point
**** If the sales are more than the points that the agent buys from Velki - Velki will take responsibility for this 40% loss

For Example: The agent bought points from Velki in 4 months – 7,500 points. Here his profit is – 7,500x40= 3,00,000 taka. But in the 5th month, it was found that the agent’s users won a lot and the agent has to sell 9,200 points to Nine Wickets. Then the agent’s loss from the profit will be 3,00,000 taka. Because the agent has to sell 7,500 points to Velki at a rate of 60 taka. The loss to sell the remaining 1,700 points will be 1,700x40= 68,000 taka, Velki will subsidize this money. The subsidy will be given so that the agent does not lose anything from his own pocket.

Then the question may arise where is the profit of being an agent? The agent’s main profit is 2% commission on the exchange. For example, 1 user made 10 bets a day. He won 5 and lost 5. At the end of the day, the user gave the agent 2% commission each time.
If an agent has 20 to 50 active players – he gets a commission of 20000 to 50000 taka per month.
In short, if you are not an agent, you will not understand the benefits of being an agent.

Conditions:

** Agent must start by buying 50,000 taka points.

** Excluding user balance – Agent must always have a balance of 1,500 points.
** If the agent balance reaches 2,000 points, the agent can sell 500 points to Velki.
** Agent – ‚Äã‚Äãcannot buy or sell less than 500 points from Velki.
** Agent must have at least 30 active players.
*** Agent will buy and sell with user for 100 taka per point.

Price of points:


** Agent will buy points from Velki for 60 taka. The price of this point may decrease due to the following reasons.
59 taka: If the agent wants his points to be 59 taka. From the day his points are 59 taka, from that day his points price will be fixed at 59 taka. The purchase and sale are the same in both cases. Velki will not be responsible for the profit and loss of the agent. No matter how much the agent sells and how much he buys, the price will be the same in both cases at 59 taka.
58 taka: If the agent wants his points to be 58 taka. From the day his points are 58 taka, the price of his points is fixed at 58 taka. In this case, the agent can only buy points from Velki - but cannot sell any points to Velki. This point rate is only for local agents - not for online agents.

Bkash/Nagad/Rocket:


** There are many people who do not have their own Bkash agent or consider it risky to transact with their own Bkash agent. In that case, you can use the company's Bkash agent. If you use the company's Bkash - Nagad agent - then the price of the point will be 65 taka.

What are the reasons why the agent can be canceled:
** One thing needs to be informed. If the agent has a habit of playing by himself - then refrain from becoming an agent. If the agent is ever caught playing by himself - then the agent account will be blocked immediately and all transactions will be stopped. If the agent wants to place a bet on an ID himself - then Velki must be informed about that ID in advance.
** If a user's points are withdrawn without informing the user.
** If the money is not paid to the user within 1 hour of withdrawing points from the user's account.
** If the user password is changed without informing the user.
